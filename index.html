About Dr. Leena

A Journey of Purpose and Impact

Dr. Leena Al-Mujahed is a medical laboratory doctor, social entrepreneur, and visionary leader dedicated to empowering women and creating sustainable change. Her journey bridges healthcare, humanitarian advocacy, and cultural diplomacy, bringing hope and opportunity to communities that need it most.

From founding the Yemeni Refugee Women Organization to establishing MyFashionChamber Malaysia, Dr. Leena has designed impactful platforms that support vulnerable women while elevating creative talent on the global stage.

  • Founder & Director, Yemeni Refugee Women Organization (YRWO)
  • Founder & Chairperson, International Fashion Chamber Malaysia
  • UNHCR Community Representative
  • Director, Arab Youth Together Malaysia
  • Founder, Maison de Lina - Luxury Modest Fashion Brand
  • UN Representative for Women's Advocacy
